Our Story

Aspen Carpet Cleaning, LLC was established in 1979. Todd started his carpet cleaning career in 1986, with this company. After 33 years of working with this company, he and his wife, Katie bought the business. We are still a small family owned carpet cleaning business. We also offer additional services such as tile cleaning, carpet repair, flood restoration and more. We service the central Ohio area.

We would like to thank you for considering us for your carpet needs.
